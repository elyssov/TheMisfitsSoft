01 · NO MORE RUBBER

The apple looked perfect.

It was rubber.

That is where games have been going for years. Rubber worlds. Rubber violence. Rubber romance. Rubber danger. Heroes with no body, no faith, no family, no blood under the fingernails.

We make games that feel authored, dangerous, specific, adult and real. The world decides. The story decides. The character decides.

THE POINT IS NOT TO MAKE “AI CONTENT.”
THE POINT IS TO MAKE GAMES ALIVE AGAIN.
